HOW IT STARTED?


On January 27, 1989 Wily Gonzalez’ dream of owning his own restaurant finally came true when he partnered with his son David and his daughter-in-law Laura to open RESTAURANT THE GUCCI. More than 30 years later his legacy of hard work, service with a smile, delicious recipes, and great value, are still alive and well. Wily’s passion for preparing great Mexican food was passed on to his son David who continues to honor his father’s legacy by preparing some of the best Mexican cuisine in Utah.
